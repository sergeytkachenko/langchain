


 All business processes belonging to your organization are stored in the
 **process library** 
 , which enables you to:
 


* Easily navigate the entire list of business processes.
* Add new business processes to your library by
 
[creating](/docs/7-16/user/bpm_tools/collaborative_business_processes_design_in_studio_creatio/create_a_process/creating_business_processes#HT_chapter_process_designer_studio_cases) 

 or
 
[importing](/docs/7-16/user/bpm_tools/collaborative_business_processes_design_in_studio_creatio/import_and_export/import_/_export#HT_chapter_export_import_studio) 

 them
* Organize business processes in a hierarchical structure
* Search for processes and folders



 Click
 ![process_library_button.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/process_library_button.png)
 to toggle the
 **process library panel** 
 . A list of all folders and processes that belong to your organization will be displayed on the left-hand side of the application.
 




![Open process library](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/open_process_library.GIF)



 Create a business process
---------------------------



 Click
 ![add_icon.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/add_icon.png)
 to add a new folders or processes, as well as
 
[import](/docs/7-16/user/bpm_tools/collaborative_business_processes_design_in_studio_creatio/import_and_export/import_/_export#HT_chapter_export_import_studio) 

 \*.bpmn files to the library.
 





 Note.
 
 In the process library, the root “All processes” folder is created by default and cannot be modified.
 





![Create new folder](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/create_new_folder.GIF)



 Configure the folder structure
--------------------------------



 You can organize your folders by creating a folder tree.
 



 Select the root folder, click
 ![add_icon00001.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/add_icon00001.png)
 , and select “Folder” to
 **create a subordinate folder** 
 .
 




![Create subordinate folder](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/create_subordinate_folder.GIF)



 Click
 ![add_icon00002.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/add_icon00002.png)
 and select “Process” to
 **add a new process**
 to a specific folder, select the folder.
 




![Add process](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/add_process.GIF)



 Drag and drop processes (or folders) to
 **move them** 
 from one location to another. Moving an entire folder will also move all its contents.
 




![Move folder](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/move_folder.GIF)



 To
 **delete or rename a folder / process** 
 , click
 ![process_library_folder_menu.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/process_library_folder_menu.png)
 and select the necessary option in the list. Additionally, you can
 
[import](/docs/7-16/user/bpm_tools/collaborative_business_processes_design_in_studio_creatio/import_and_export/import_/_export#HT_chapter_export_import_studio) 

 a process from a \*.bpmn file or add it directly into the selected folder.
 




![Delete folder](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/delete_folder.png)





 Attention.
 
 Deleting a folder will delete all its contents, i.e. all other processes and folders in it.
 




 Find a process
----------------



 To search for folders and processes, click
 ![search_icon.png](/guides/sites/default/files/documentation/user/ru/studio_free/BPMonlineHelp/chapter_process_library_studio/search_icon.png)
 and start typing your request. The
 **process library** 
 panel will show a list of folders and processes that match search criteria.
 



 Click a list item to open it.
 




![Search library](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-12/search_library.GIF)
