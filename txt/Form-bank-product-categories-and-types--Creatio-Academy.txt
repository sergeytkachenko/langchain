


 Use the
 
 Product categories and types
 
 lookup to form bank product categories and types, as well as add
 [document packages](https://academy.creatio.com/documents?id=2156#title-2924-8) 
 to generate automatically in the product sales conditions. The lookup also contains
 [criteria](https://academy.creatio.com/documents?id=2156#title-2924-17) 
 by which a manager can modify the product sales conditions.
 



 The product category in the
 
 Product categories and types
 
 lookup includes several product types (Fig. 1).
 




 Fig. 1 – The structure of the [Product categories and types] lookup
 

![Categories and types lookup](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-11/section_lookups_categories_and_types.png)



 Create a product category
---------------------------



 To add a product category to a lookup:
 


1. Click
 ![system_designer.png](/docs/sites/default/files/inline-images/system_designer.png)
 to open the System Designer.
2. Click the [Lookups] link in the [System setup] block.
3. Open the [Product categories and types] lookup.
4. Click the [New category] button (Fig. 2).
 

 Fig. 2 – Adding a product category to the [Product categories and types] lookup
 

![Adding new category](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-11/section_lookups_new_category.png)
5. Enter the product category name (for example, “Loan”) and select the class (for example, “Product“) (Fig. 3).
 

 Fig. 3 – Example of adding a new product category
 

![Create category](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-11/section_lookups_create_category.png)
6. Click [Save].
7. Add other product categories to the lookup in the same manner.



 Add product category
----------------------



 To add products to a category:
 


1. Select the needed category in the area on the right side of the lookup page and click the
 ![btn_com_menu_gear_1.png](/docs/sites/default/files/inline-images/btn_com_menu_gear_1.png)
 button.
2. Select the [New type] command (Fig. 4).
 

 Fig. 4 – Adding a new product type to a category
 

![Add a new product type to a category](/docs/sites/en/files/2020-11/section_lookups_new_type.png)
3. Enter the product type name (for example, “Consumer lending”).
4. Save the changes.
5. Add other product types of this category in the same manner.
