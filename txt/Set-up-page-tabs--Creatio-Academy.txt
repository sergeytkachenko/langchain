


 By default, each new page contains
 
 Attachments and notes
 
 and
 
 Feed
 
 tabs. You can add new tabs, edit the existing ones, as well as manage the order in which they are displayed on the page (Fig. 1).
 





 Fig. 1
 

 Page tab setup area
 

![scr_section_wizard_tabs_setup_area.png](/guides/sites/en/files/documentation/user/en/ui_business_logic_customization/BPMonlineHelp/set_up_page_tabs/scr_section_wizard_tabs_setup_area.png)



 To
 **add a new tab** 
 on the page:
 


1. Open a section, e.g.,
 
 Accounts
 
 .
2. Click
 
 View
 
 →
 **Open Section Wizard** 
 .
3. In the “Section pages” block of the Section Wizard:
 


	1. if you have only one edit page in your section, click
	 **Edit page;**
	2. if you have several edit pages in your section, click
	 **the link of a corresponding page** 
	 in the list.
4. Click
 ![btn_com_add_tab.png](/guides/sites/en/files/documentation/user/en/ui_business_logic_customization/BPMonlineHelp/set_up_page_tabs/btn_com_add_tab.png)
 in the right part of the tab setup area.
5. In the opened window, enter a caption for the new tab and click
 
 Save
 
 .
 



 As a result, a new empty tab will be added. On a tab, you can
 [add field groups](https://academy.creatio.com/documents?product=administration&ver=7&id=1401) 
 ,
 [fields,](https://academy.creatio.com/documents?product=administration&ver=7&id=1399) 
 and
 [details](https://academy.creatio.com/documents?product=administration&ver=7&id=2030) 
 .
 



 To
 **rename the currently open tab** 
 , click
 ![btn_chapter_content_designer_htlm_element_edit.png](/guides/sites/en/files/documentation/user/en/ui_business_logic_customization/BPMonlineHelp/set_up_page_tabs/btn_chapter_content_designer_htlm_element_edit.png)
 .
 



 To
 **modify the tab content** 
 , add a field group and drag the required fields from the
 
 New column
 
 and
 
 Existing columns
 
 areas. You can add details on the tab in a similar way.
 



 To
 **change the tab position** 
 , hover the cursor over the tab title, click and drag it to the required place.



 To
 **delete a tab** 
 , select it and click
 ![btn_delete_record_on_dtl.png](/guides/sites/en/files/documentation/user/en/ui_business_logic_customization/BPMonlineHelp/set_up_page_tabs/btn_delete_record_on_dtl.png)
 in the right part of the tab setup area. If
 **you accidentally delete** 
 one of the default tabs, you can only restore it by discarding the current changes in the Section Wizard. There are two ways you can do it when you delete the tab:
 


1. Exit the Section Wizard by closing the window and open it again. You will see the accidentally deleted tab reappear in the tab setup area.
2. Refresh the page by pressing F5 as soon as you delete the tab (Fig. 2).
 





 Fig. 2
 

 Restoring the deleted default tab in the Section Wizard
 

![chapter_section_wizard_restore_tab.gif](/guides/sites/en/files/documentation/user/en/ui_business_logic_customization/BPMonlineHelp/set_up_page_tabs/chapter_section_wizard_restore_tab.gif)





 Attention.
 
 If you delete the default
 
 Feed
 
 tab in your section and save the changes in the Section Wizard, you will only be able to restore it using the developer means.
